Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

At 0to60timer.com, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our 0 to 60 timer web application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use our service. The app is completely anonymous.

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our service, we may automatically collect:

  • Analytics Data: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This includes information like page views, session duration, device type, and general geographic location (country/city level). This data is anonymized and aggregated.
  • Technical Information: Browser type, operating system, and device information to ensure compatibility and improve our service.

Location and Sensor Data

Important: While our app requests access to your device's GPS location and motion sensors (accelerometer, gyroscope), this data is processed entirely on your device. We do not transmit, store, or have access to:

  • Your GPS coordinates or location data
  • Your speed or acceleration measurements
  • Your performance run data
  • Any sensor readings from your device

All performance tracking happens locally in your browser. Your location and sensor data never leaves your device.

How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used to:

  • Understand how users interact with our site to improve the service
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Analyze aggregate usage patterns and trends
  • Ensure the app works properly across different devices and browsers

Data Storage

Your performance run data (0-60 times, quarter mile times, etc.) is stored locally in your browser's local storage. This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is not transmitted to our servers
  • Can be cleared by clearing your browser data
  • Is not accessible to us or any third parties

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Google Analytics: To track anonymous usage statistics
  • Local Storage: To save your performance runs on your device

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect functionality.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For anonymous usage analytics. Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Fonts: For typography. Google Fonts privacy: https://developers.google.com/fonts/faq/privacy

Data Security

Since we don't collect or store your location, speed, or performance data, there's minimal risk to your privacy. The analytics data we collect is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Use our service without providing any personal information
  • Clear your locally stored run data at any time by clearing browser data
  • Opt out of analytics by using browser extensions like uBlock Origin or Privacy Badger
  • Request information about what data we collect (which is minimal and anonymous)

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used our service, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

contact@0to60timer.com

Summary

Bottom line: We don't collect, store, or have access to your location, speed, or performance data. All tracking happens locally on your device. We only collect anonymous analytics to improve the service. Your privacy is protected.
