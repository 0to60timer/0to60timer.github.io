How to Enable Sensors on Your Device

üì± Device Motion & Fitness

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Scroll down and tap Privacy & Security
  3. Tap Motion & Fitness
  4. Turn on Fitness Tracking
  5. Scroll down and find Safari (or your browser)
  6. Turn on the toggle next to your browser
  7. Return to this page and click "Request Access" above

üìç Location Services

  1. Open the Settings app
  2. Tap Privacy & Security
  3. Tap Location Services
  4. Turn on Location Services at the top
  5. Scroll down and find Safari (or your browser)
  6. Tap on your browser and select While Using App
  7. Return to this page and click "Request Access" above

üì± Motion Sensors

  1. Open Chrome (or your browser)
  2. Tap the three dots menu in the top-right
  3. Tap Settings
  4. Tap Site settings
  5. Tap Motion sensors
  6. Turn on Motion sensors
  7. Return to this page and click "Request Access" above

Alternative: When prompted by this website, tap Allow for motion access.

üìç Location Services

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Tap Location (or Privacy ‚Üí Location)
  3. Turn on Use location
  4. Open Chrome (or your browser)
  5. Tap the three dots menu ‚Üí Settings
  6. Tap Site settings ‚Üí Location
  7. Turn on Location
  8. Return to this page and click "Request Access" above

Alternative: When prompted by this website, tap Allow for location access.
