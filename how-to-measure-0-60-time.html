How to Measure 0-60 Time

Professional acceleration testing using just your smartphone

What is 0-60 Time?

The 0-60 mph time measures how quickly a vehicle can accelerate from a complete stop to 60 miles per hour. It's one of the most important performance metrics for any car, providing a standardized way to compare acceleration capabilities across different vehicles.

Why Measure 0-60 Time?

  • Performance Verification: Confirm your car's actual performance matches manufacturer claims
  • Modification Testing: Measure improvements after performance upgrades
  • Vehicle Comparison: Compare acceleration between different cars
  • Track Progress: Monitor performance changes over time

How to Measure Your 0-60 Time

Step 1: Preparation

  1. Find a safe, legal location (closed course or empty straight road)
  2. Ensure your phone is fully charged
  3. Mount your phone securely in the vehicle
  4. Check tire pressure and fuel level

Step 2: Set Up 0to60Timer.com

  1. Visit 0to60Timer.com/tracker
  2. Grant location and motion sensor permissions
  3. The app will automatically calibrate
  4. Wait for GPS signal to stabilize

Step 3: Perform the Test

  1. Come to a complete stop
  2. Ensure the speed reads 0 mph
  3. Accelerate as hard as safely possible
  4. The timer automatically starts when motion is detected
  5. Continue accelerating through 60 mph
  6. The app records your time automatically

Step 4: Review Results

Your 0-60 time is displayed immediately, along with:

  • Exact time to 60 mph
  • Speed chart showing acceleration curve
  • Best time comparison
  • Historical data

Tips for Accurate Results

  • Multiple Runs: Perform 3-5 runs and take the best result
  • Consistent Conditions: Test in similar weather and temperature
  • Phone Placement: Mount phone securely, avoid movement
  • Launch Technique: Practice consistent launches
  • GPS Signal: Ensure strong GPS signal before testing
